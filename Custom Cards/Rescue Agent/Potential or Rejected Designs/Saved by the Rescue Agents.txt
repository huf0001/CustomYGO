TECHNICAL----------------------------------------------------------------------

Name: Saved by the Rescue Agents
Card Type: Trap
Card Subtype(s): Counter
Effect(s): ① When a monster(s) would be Summoned, OR when a Spell/Trap Card, or monster effect, is activated that includes an effect that Special Summons a monster(s); negate the Summon or activation, and if you do, shuffle it into the Deck. You must control a "Rescue Agent" monster to activate and to resolve this effect.
Last Updated: 31/05/2024
