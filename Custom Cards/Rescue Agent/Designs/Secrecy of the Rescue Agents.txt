TECHNICAL----------------------------------------------------------------------

Name: Secrecy of the Rescue Agents
Card Type: Trap
Card Subtype(s): Counter
Effect(s): ① When a Spell/Trap Card, or monster effect is activated that targets a "Rescue Agent" monster you control; negate the activation, and if you do, shuffle it into the Deck. ② If this card is in your GY, except the turn it was sent there: You can banish this card from your GY, then target 1 "Rescue Agent" monster you control; it is unaffected by your opponent's card effects until the end of this turn.
Last Updated: 31/05/2024
