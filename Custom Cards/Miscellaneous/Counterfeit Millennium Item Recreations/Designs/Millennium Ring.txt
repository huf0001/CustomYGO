Name: Millennium Ring
Card Type: Spell
Card Subtype(s): Normal
Effect(s): ① After this card's activation, it remains on the field. ② If a monster you control attacks an opponent's monster, your opponent takes any battle damage you would have taken. ③ If "Millennium Ring" is destroyed and sent to your GY for the second time this Duel: You cannot activate "Millennium Ring" for the rest of the Duel.
