Neo-Spacian support effects (Cross Porter, Cross Keeper, Neo Space monsters)
	DARK ① You can target 1 monster you control; send that target to the GY, and if you do, Special Summon 1 "Neo-Spacian" monster from your hand. (Cross Porter)
	FIRE ② When this card is sent to the GY: You can add 1 "Neo-Spacian" monster from your Deck to your hand. (Cross Porter)
	NEOS ③ You can discard this card; add 1 "Neo Space" from your Deck or GY to your hand. (Neo Space Pathfinder)
	EARTH ④ When this card is Normal Summoned: You can Special Summon 1 "Neo-Spacian" monster or 1 "Elemental HERO Neos" from your hand or Deck in Defense Position. You can only use this effect of this card's name once per turn. (Neo Space Connector)
	WIND ⑤ You can Tribute this card, then target 1 "Neo-Spacian" monster or 1 "Elemental HERO Neos" in your GY; Special Summon it in Defense Position. You can only use this effect of this card's name once per turn. (Neo Space Connector)
	WATER ⑥ You can send this card from your hand or field to the GY; Special Summon 1 "Elemental HERO" monster or 1 "Neo-Spacian" monster from your hand or GY, but negate its effects if Summoned from the GY. You can only use this effect of this card's name once per turn. (Cross Keeper)
	LIGHT ⑦ If you Special Summon an "Elemental HERO" Fusion Monster, while this card is in your GY (except during the Damage Step): You can banish this card; draw 2 cards, then place 1 card from your hand on the bottom of the Deck. You can only use this effect of this card's name once per turn. (Cross Keeper)
