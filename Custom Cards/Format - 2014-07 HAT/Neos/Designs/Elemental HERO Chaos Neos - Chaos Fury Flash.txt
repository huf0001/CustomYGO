TECHNICAL----------------------------------------------------------------------

Name: Elemental HERO Chaos Neos - Chaos Fury Flash
Card Type: Monster
Card Subtype(s): Fusion / Effect
Level: 9
Attribute: LIGHT DARK
Monster Type: Plant Beast Warrior
Materials: "Elemental HERO Neos" + 2 "Neo-Spacian" monsters (1 LIGHT and 1 DARK)
Effect(s): Must first be Special Summoned (from your Extra Deck) by shuffling the above cards you control into the Deck. ① Once per turn, during your Main Phase 1: You can toss a coin 3 times, then apply the following effect based on its results. ● 3 Heads: Destroy all monsters your opponent controls. ● 2 Heads: Negate the effects of all face-up monsters your opponent controls until the end of this turn. ● 1 Head: Return all monsters you control to the hand. ② During the End Phase: You can return this card to the Extra Deck; Set all face-up monsters on the field, and if you do, add 1 "Neos" monster and 2 "Neo-Spacian" monsters with different Attributes (except DARK or LIGHT) from your Deck to your hand.
ATK: 3000
DEF: 2500
