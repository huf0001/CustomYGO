CREATIVE-----------------------------------------------------------------------

Basis: The White Stone of Legend + Skeletal Dragon Felgrand
Flavour: The White Stone of Legend retrained for a Dragon Lords archetype.
Artwork: Created in GIMP using:
● The White Stone of Legend
● Ruins of the Divine Dragon Lords


COMMENTS-----------------------------------------------------------------------

The White Stone of Legend: ① If this card is sent to the GY: Add 1 "Blue-Eyes White Dragon" from your Deck to your hand.

The White Stone of Ancients: You can only use effect ② of "The White Stone of Ancients" once per turn. ① Once per turn, during the End Phase, if this card is in the GY because it was sent there this turn: You can Special Summon 1 "Blue-Eyes" monster from your Deck. ② You can banish this card from your GY, then target 1 "Blue-Eyes" monster in your GY; add it to your hand.

Skeletal Dragon Felgrand: You can only use each effect of "Skeletal Dragon Felgrand" once per turn. ① If this card is Special Summoned: You can target 1 monster your opponent controls or in their GY; banish it. ② If a monster(s) is Special Summoned from either GY, while this monster is on the field (except during the Damage Step): You can target 1 other face-up monster on the field; negate its effects until the end of this turn.
