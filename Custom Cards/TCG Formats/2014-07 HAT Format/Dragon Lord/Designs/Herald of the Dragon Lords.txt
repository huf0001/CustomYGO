COMMENTS-----------------------------------------------------------------------

Herald of Creation: ① Once per turn: You can discard 1 card, then target 1 Level 7 or higher monster in your Graveyard; add that target to your hand.

Dragon Knight of Creation: You can only use effect ③ of "Dragon Knight of Creation" once per turn. ① Increase this card's Level by 4 during your opponent's turn only. ② When this card destroys an opponent's monster by battle and sends it to the GY: You can send 1 Level 7 or 8 Dragon monster from your Deck to the GY. ③ You can send 1 card from your hand to the GY, then target 1 Level 7 or 8 Dragon monster in your GY; send this card to the GY, and if you do, Special Summon that monster.

Paladin of Felgrand: ① If this card is Normal or Special Summoned: You can equip 1 Level 7 or 8 Dragon monster from your hand or Deck to this card. ② This card is unaffected by other monsters' effects, while equipped with an Equip Card. ③ You can Tribute 1 monster and this card, then target 1 Level 7 or 8 Dragon monster in your GY; Special Summon it.

Guardian of Felgrand: ① If this card is Normal or Special Summoned: You can equip 1 Level 7 or 8 Dragon-Type monster from your hand or Graveyard to this card. ② This card gains ATK and DEF equal to half the ATK and DEF of the monster equipped to it by this effect. ③ You can Tribute 1 monster and this card, then target 1 Level 7 or 8 Dragon-Type monster in your Graveyard; Special Summon it.
