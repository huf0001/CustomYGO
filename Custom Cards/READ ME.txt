Version number A.B.C.D

Increment A when:

	Publishing a new custom card type.
	Overhauling a custom card type.

Increment B when:

	Publishing a new custom archetype or series, including TCG erratas or counterfeit card re-creations.
	Overhauling an archetype or series, including TCG erratas or counterfeit card re-creations.
	Adding new support that makes an existing custom card that was a single part of an archetype or series, including TCG erratas or counterfeit card re-creations.

Increment C when:

	Publishing a new single, including TCG erratas or counterfeit card re-creations.
	Adding new support for an archetype or series that has already had custom cards published.
	
Increment D when:

	Errata-ing a previously published card(s).
	Restructuring Published Cards.
