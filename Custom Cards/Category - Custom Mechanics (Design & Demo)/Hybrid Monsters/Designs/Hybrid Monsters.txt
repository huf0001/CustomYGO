TECHNICAL----------------------------------------------------------------------

Name: Hybrid Monsters
Card Type: Rule
Rules Text: ① Hybrid Monsters are monsters with multiple Monster Card types (Ritual, Fusion, Synchro, Dark Synchro, Xyz, or Link). They are always treated as all of those types. E.g. a Ritual Fusion Monster is always both a Ritual Monster and a Fusion Monster. ② Hybrid Monsters are put in the Extra Deck, even if they are Hybrid Ritual Monsters. ③ A Hybrid Monster can be properly Special Summoned (from your Extra Deck) via the correct Summoning procedure for any of its card types, unless it has a Summoning condition that states otherwise. E.g. a Synchro Xyz Monster can be Synchro Summoned or Xyz Summoned, using the materials listed for that Summoning procedure. ④ If a Hybrid Monster is Special Summoned using the procedure for one of its card types, effects that activate when a monster is Special Summoned with a different Summoning procedure cannot be activated. E.g. if a Fusion Link Monster is Link Summoned, effects that activate when a monster is Fusion Summoned cannot be activated. ⑤ If a Hybrid Monster is moved to the hand or Main Deck, it is returned to the Extra Deck face-down instead. ⑥ Dark Synchro Monster, Xyz Monster, and Link Monster Hybrid Monsters all have a Negative Level, Rank, or Link Rating property, in addition to the Level or equivalent properties of the other component card types. Their values do not need to be the same. E.g. a Synchro Xyz Monster could be both Level 6 and Rank 4, while a Dark Synchro Link Monster could be Negative Level 7 and Link 3. A Ritual, Fusion, or Synchro Monster that is also a Dark Synchro monster has both a Level and a Negative Level. ⑦ Hybrid Link Monsters do not have DEF points and cannot be changed to Defense position.
