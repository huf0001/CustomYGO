COMMENTS-----------------------------------------------------------------------

Alternative name: Elemental HERO Astro Neos

Attack name: Ultimate Meteor Flash. As Cosmo Neos is visually a combo of Chaos, Magma, and Storm Neos, its attack name uses elements from the attack names of all three: Ultimate Typhoon (Storm Neos), Meteor Meltdown (Magma Neos), and Chaos Fury Flash (Chaos Neos).
