Files and assets used to create Container Monsters:

	- Card images exported from https://www.cardmaker.net/yugioh.
	- Card frame assets and fonts used by https://www.cardmaker.net/yugioh, available at https://www.cardmaker.net/uploads/cardmaker_resources/.
	- Fonts used by https://lauqerm.github.io/ygocarder/, available at https://github.com/lauqerm/ygocarder/tree/main/public/asset/font.
	- Font: DF 隷書体 Std W5 (listed as DFLeiSho Std W5 in GIMP), available at https://eng.fontke.com/font/188509001/.
	- Colour sample from the card frame of the Japanese anime verison of Magical Hats, available at https://yugipedia.com/wiki/Card_Gallery:Magical_Hats#/media/File:MagicalHats-JP-Anime-DM.png.
