Files and assets used to create Normal Monsters (Modern and Series 1 & 2 Style):

	- Yugipedia's Attribute and Level icon .SVG files, available at https://yugipedia.com/wiki/Attribute and https://yugipedia.com/wiki/Level respectively.
	- Card images exported from https://www.cardmaker.net/yugioh.
	- Card frame assets and fonts used by https://www.cardmaker.net/yugioh, available at https://www.cardmaker.net/uploads/cardmaker_resources/.
	- Card frame assets and fonts used by https://lauqerm.github.io/ygocarder/, available at https://github.com/lauqerm/ygocarder/tree/main/public/asset.
	- Series 1 and 2 card frames as reference, available at https://yugipedia.com/wiki/Card_layout.
